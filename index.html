Who we are...

In a world desperately searching for answers, we believe that Christ and His Word are the ultimate source for lasting hope.

Our central purpose is very clear... to display the glory of God. From our corporate worship, to our outreach, to our men and women's gatherings, to our family ministry, Jesus Christ is our greatest treasure that we desire to delight in and make known.

Where we're ministering...

Care group info link

COHBC Care Groups intentionally shepherd God's people through discovery Bible study, authentic relationships and caring prayer in a highly relational setting.

Starting September 20th these groups meet weekly throughout the Spokane region and are the primary way that the people of COHBC are cared for in all aspects of life.
